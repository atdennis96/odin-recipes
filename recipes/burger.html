Lasagna Recipe

A delicious piece of lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Put Pasta in the bottom of the pot
  2. Add a layer of meat
  3. Add a layer of sauce
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until you have 5 layers
  5. Bake in oven at 450*F for 30 minutes
  6. Let sit for 5 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
